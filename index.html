1. Information and Data Storage

At Cursor Roar, we follow the principle of data minimization:

  • We do not collect or store on external servers any personal data that can directly identify you (such as name, email address, or phone number).
  • To ensure your gaming experience, MAGNETIC CHAOS stores data locally on your device. This includes level progress, high scores, and configuration preferences. This data remains on your device and can be deleted if you clear the cache or uninstall the application.

2. Third-Party Services

To keep the game free, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Although we do not store your data on our own servers, the AdMob SDK may automatically collect and process certain information necessary for the advertising ecosystem:

  • Android Advertising ID.
  • Performance information.
  • Ad clicks and app launch events.

You can consult the Google AdMob Privacy Policy to understand how they manage this information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. International Compliance (GDPR and CCPA)

  • European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom: We comply with the EU User Consent Policy. Before ads are loaded, you will see a Consent Management Platform (CMP) form. You can modify or revoke your preferences at any time from the game settings.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): We do not "sell" your personal information in the traditional sense. Any sharing of advertising identifiers with Google AdMob is done for the operational purposes of the advertising service.

4. Protection of Minors (COPPA)

MAGNETIC CHAOS is a game intended for a general audience.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
  • If we detect that the user is a minor, the game is configured to request AdMob to serve only non-personalized (contextual) ads, ensuring compliance with the COPPA Act and Google Play Families Policies.

5. Data Security

Although we do not manage user databases in the cloud, we are committed to maintaining software integrity to protect local data stored on your device and ensuring that third-party integrations are secure.

As a user, you have the right to:
